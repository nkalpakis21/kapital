Mission

OpenChain Ventures is a crypto-native venture capital fund designed to invest in early-stage tech startups through a fully on-chain, transparent, and globally accessible framework. We aim to radically improve the VC model using blockchain capabilities.

Vision

To create the most liquid, inclusive, and founder-aligned investment ecosystem for the next generation of tech innovation.

Fund Structure

  • Tokenized LP Shares: LPs receive fungible tokens representing their stake in the fund, tradable after a vesting period.
  • NAV Tracking: Smart contracts and oracles update the Net Asset Value (NAV) and IRR in real-time.
  • Automated Distributions: Profits are streamed to LP wallets via smart contracts using protocols like Superfluid.

Investment Strategy

  • Focus on Web3, AI, infra, and fintech startups.
  • Global founder onboarding via wallet-based identity.
  • Milestone-based capital disbursement using vesting contracts.

Governance

  • LPs vote on investment decisions using governance tokens.
  • Delegate-based voting possible to reduce friction.
  • Community bounties for diligence, research, and scouting.

Differentiators

  • On-chain transparency.
  • Community co-investment pools.
  • Instant liquidity for LPs.
  • Founder reputation tied to on-chain identity.

Regulatory & Compliance

  • KYC/AML via on-chain attestations.
  • Jurisdictional structuring in Switzerland/Singapore.
